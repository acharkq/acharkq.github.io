Biography

I am a PhD student at NExT++, where I am supervised by Prof. Chua Tat-Seng and co-supervised by Prof. Kawaguchi Kenji. I am also fortunate to be mentored by Prof. Wang Xiang at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). My research primarily focuses on AI for Science, particularly Multi-modal Generative Modeling for emerging scientific modalities such as molecules and proteins. Additionally, I have a keen interest in Graph Neural Networks and Natural Language Processing.
